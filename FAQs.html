How do you improve the user experience of a website?

Firstly I would propose that at a bare minimum that the website is fully funcitional without bugs or inconsistencies. No matter how athestically pleaseing you make a website, if it doesnt work and the user cannot navigate through it with ease it is already a failure. Another important aspect would be that the website's aim is clear and understood by the user. If the the prevous two points are made then I would say try to create something unique. There are thousands of amazing websites and web designers alike so making something different and memorable would be fantistic in improving the user experience and inspire them to return to your website.
